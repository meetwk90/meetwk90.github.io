眼神，自动对焦。

在你的世界中心，肆意挥洒我的生命。

爱，心甘陪伴，情愿骄纵。

大概如是。


Our visions focusing in one.

In your life, living mine.

Love is, following one's heart, meanwhile be willing to free this man.

Perhaps this is the way, we give and gain.
